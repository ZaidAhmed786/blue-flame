Blue Flame Propane: We Bring the Heat!

Residential and commercial propane in Michigan

Blue Flame Propane, owned by the Foster family, has been keeping folks safe and warm in Michigan since 1989. We are a hometown, customer-focused business — making decisions based on what is best for our customers. Since we are family-owned and Michigan-based, we are operated by the very people you know and trust. Our reputation is directly related to our customers' satisfaction, guaranteeing you the most reliable, courteous service available.

Blue Flame Propane has grown from one location in Lexington, Michigan, to ten locations serving the majority of the state. Blue Flame Propane locations include offices in Richmond, Lexington, Lapeer, Midland, Harrison, West Branch, Bridgeport, Pewamo and Mio.

We carry different size LP tanks to best suit your needs. We have a free lease on different sized propane tanks. Tank sizes range from 1,000 gallons to 500, 330, 250 and 120 gallons.

Blue Flame Propane offers competitive pricing. We don't only have low prices on first fills – we are competitive all year round.

We're also pleased to offer 24 hour live emergency after hours service.

How to Choose a Heating Professional
Check their reputation.

Ask your friends, family and neighbors about their propane suppliers.

Check their experience.

How long have they been in business? How many customers do they deliver to each year? Will they be around to continue providing me with service?

What about their employees?

Are they well trained? Are the service technicians up-to-date on the latest installation and service procedures? Do the employees regularly attend seminars and training schools? Are they certified through the Gas Association Program?

Can they perform Gas Checks?

Gas Check is a licensed voluntary program to inspect the residential propane system (lines, furnace, appliances, etc.) to ensure safety.

Blue Flame Propane employees attend training classes certified by the National Propane Gas Association (NPGA) annually. Customers can be assured that our employees are properly trained to perform their jobs knowledgeably, confidently, and most of all safely.
